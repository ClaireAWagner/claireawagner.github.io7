Crane Guitar

Original guitar body and control design achieves the dynamic look of wild shaped heavy metal guitars while providing an ergonomic experience for practice and performance. Shape is inspired by the origami crane. Western red alder wood and stainless steel, body is 22 inches long.

Magic Smart Box

Absurd object parodying the wide range of extremely minimal “smart” home products available. It condescends to the user, presenting them with an “impenetrable interface” with missing and incorrect affordances in which it is unclear how to do a task framed as simple. Laser cut chip board and Arduino, 12 inch square.

Collaborative Drum



Prototype of new musical instrument concept to keep drummers in time without auditory cues and to teach rhythm. Hits are felt on the lap of the person opposite encouraging connection through touch. Poplar wood and electrical metallic tube, length is three feet.

Home Lampshade

Lighting design for the Barcelona Pavilion from the imagined perspective of the time. Aesthetics inspired by Frank Lloyd Wright’s trapezoids and the bright colors of De Stijl, with a practical, flat-packing, fastener and adhesive-free assembly process. Laser cut acrylic, 18 inches wide.

Circuit Speaker

Rustic and minimal speaker design shows off the internal electronics of a project to design and create a circuit board. Reused crate wood, copper, electronic parts, thin printed fabric, 13 inches wide.

Maker Bread

A breadmaker for the make-it-yourself obsessed. To interest a demographic that prides itself on effort and creation in an automatic product, Maker Bread uses relatable processes and practical materials. Bread-slice forms show function and glowing internal electronics feel like vintage vacuum tubes. Bakelite and brushed stainless steel, 18 inches wide.

Human Plant Interface

Prototype tower concept to provide light and aid in the care of house plants. Tower communicates with smartphone and indicators tell the user when to water, feed, or go to an app, and promotes the human need to nurture. Form inspired by bamboo shoots. PVC pipe, tower height is ten inches.

Haptic Desk Toy

A playful object in desk-worthy materials. Place your hand on the shallow peak, flick the free spinning beads and release restlessness. Form inspired by a dune. Western red alder wood and brass tube, longest side six inches.

Mind Set Table Setting

Table setting for children to instill the values of eating whole ingredients and greens. Craftsman inspired embossed plate graphics show the parts of a healthful dish and their origins in the Earth. Utensils are suited to vegetables, fish, grains and stews. Forms based on Shaker simplicity and homestead techniques. Enamel on stainless steel, child sized eight inch plate and six inch utensils.

The Modern Legend of Time

A zine about the value of time. An overworked character fatefully wishes time to stop and learns the beauty and potential of new moments. Social media motif pokes fun at instant gratification and wasted time. User manual included. Photo paper and clear plastic, smartphone sized.

Context

Context is a desk divider that combats visual distraction and unwanted lighting in open-style offices. The form began as an optimization in Rhino Grasshopper for view and daylight blocking and was modified through two versions based on the results of user testing.
